Weddings
The two shall become one flesh



The United Methodist Church of Yucaipa is a beautiful place for a wedding.


The church has a beautiful stone sanctuary with many stained glass windows. It has charm that is not found in many contemporary churches. The stone sanctuary was built in the 1940's, now with modern heating and air conditioning and an excellent sound / multi-media projection system.

There is a Fellowship Hall with an adjoining kitchen, lovely grounds with stately pines, cedars, and stonework, and many excellent photographic locations. The facility can accomodate several hundred people. For information about using the church facilities for your wedding, please contact the church office.
